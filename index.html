Halloween Hunt


Hershey’s Halloween Hunt is an augmented reality game created to provide children and their families with a safe trick-or-treating experience.


About Demo

About

As a result of COVID-19, children nationwide cannot safely attend school in person, play sports, or have fun dressing up for Halloween and going trick-or-treating with their friends. This takes a significant toll on the physical and mental health of the kids, as they cannot see their friends and are much less active always being inside.


Hershey’s Halloween Hunt is designed to encourage kids to be active and provide an enjoyable experience collecting Halloween candy, while simultaneously keeping safety at the highest priority.


Children play using a smartphone, and the game utilizes geo-location technology to display an augmented reality map of the area surrounding the user. From an in-app screen that displays the user’s location and where the nearest candies are relative to this location, children can choose which candy they want to go after and then walk towards that place in real life.

Upon arrival, a monster that is associated with the type of candy being collected will pop up on the screen for the child to face an AR “monster encounter.” In order to get the treat, one piece of a Hershey’s chocolate bar, the monster will give the child a task that encourages physical activity that must be completed in order to gain the treat. Tasks will range from 10 jumping jacks to 10 star jumps to walking 100 steps. When the task is completed, the user will earn the treat and it will be added to their candy collection.

The game continues as they can then return to the map screen to find the next candy near them. Once players collect 12 pieces of one type of candy, they can choose to trade it in for an actual Hershey’s chocolate bar sent in the mail, providing tangible candy like kids could get from actual trick-or-treating.

With Hershey’s Halloween Hunt, our goal is to get kids up and moving while simultaneously providing a socially distanced, but enjoyable, alternative Halloween experience. We are committed to utilizing our skills in graphic design, webpage and app creation, video production and app creation to encourage exercise this season of quarantine.

Key Features

  1. Real-world gameplay that gives children the opportunity to safely trick-or-treat by utilizing augmented reality technology through a mobile app
  2. Creatively engages kids while also promoting physical activity
  3. Location-based, AR “monster encounters”
  4. Earn a treat, or 1 Hershey’s chocolate piece, from defeating monster that can be redeemed for physical candy sent in the mail
  5. Ability to create and customize an account and avatar; with candy collection unlocking character upgrades
  6. In user creation, asks for allergy information to ensure that candy sent to users does not contain or have any contact with allergens.
  7. Tracks steps taken, and each 1,000 steps kids earn an additional piece of candy to encourage movement even if they aren’t able to defeat as many monsters
  8. Alternative accessibility tasks for monster encounters for kids who are unable to physically complete the task
  9. Designed to encourage kids to continue to playing to collect all different types of candy to earn more sent in the mail
  10. Portable and accessible, as all that is required is a smartphone with geolocation capabilities
